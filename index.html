Guide
How to Play
1
Explore the Map
Open the Map tab and tap any era — from 40,000-year-old Cave Art to today's AI Art. Each era unlocks a full chapter of history.
2
Swipe Through Art Cards
Each era has 6 cards covering key artworks, figures, and innovations. Swipe horizontally to read each one. The dots at the bottom show your progress.
3
Collect Artifacts
Tap the artifact chips listed in each era to discover and collect them. Artifacts come in 4 rarities: Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary. View your collection in the Collection tab.
4
Take the Challenge
Every era ends with a 3-question quiz. Answer correctly to earn XP — up to 250 XP per perfect score. You need a perfect score to mark the era as complete.
5
Build Your Art DNA
As you complete eras, your Art DNA profile builds up — showing which art periods resonate most with you. Export it as a PNG to share.
6
Stamp Your Passport
Complete an era to earn its passport stamp. Your Art Passport tracks your journey and shows how much of human creativity you've explored.
7
Unlock Badges
Explore enough eras, collect enough artifacts, and keep your quiz streak alive to unlock achievement badges. Some are hidden — keep exploring to find them.
